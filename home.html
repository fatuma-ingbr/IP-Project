About Me

For my September 2017 internship, I interned with the African Leadership College (ALC) Mauritius. ALC Mauritius is a tertiary institution whose mission is to transform Africa by empowering its future leaders.

While at ALC, I interned as a software engineering teacher assistant. my main role was to assist with the delivery of the Software Engineering module. General tasks included participating in session planning, attending weekly reviews, and providing academic support to students.

Furthermore, as side projects, I helped with the design of the computer science lab. I also worked on documenting the software engineering learning outcomes. For my personal project, I collaborated with fellow computing department interns to work on a software development project.

The purpose of the project was to develop a platform that will visualize the learning outcomes of a given module. This project was not completed during the internship period due to its complexity. However, we agreed to continue working on the project even after the internship.

About ALU

The African Leadership University (ALU) is a network of tertiaty institutions with a vision to tranform Africa through re-inventing the delivery of higher education. ALU plans to achieve its vision through educating its future leaders by using an innovate learning model.

The institution has two physical campuses; ALU Rwanda and the African Leadership College (ALC) Mauritius.

In additon to its undergraduate programmes, ALU offer graduate programmes under its School of Wildlife Conservation and School of Business.

ALU Programs

This is the African Leadership University Rwanda. ALU Rwanda offers undergraduate degrees in Computer Science, Entrepreneurship, Global Challenges as well as International Business and Trade.

Retrieved from ALU Rwanda

This is the ALU School of Business (ALUSB). ALUSB is the first and only pan-African school of business. The ALUSB education is unique in a way that is tailored to the African context.

Retrieved from ALUSB

This is the African Leadership College Mauritius. ALC offers degrees in Business, Computing, Electrical Engineering as well as Social Science in partnership with GCU. This is the first ALU campus!

The ALU School Wildlife Conservation is a graduate programme that equip students with necessary skills that will solve global and African conservation challenges.
